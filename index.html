Annotation guidelines

Positive

Use when the sentence expresses praise, delight, satisfaction, recommendation, relief, or clear approval.

Example I love this feature because it saves time.

Negative

Use when the sentence expresses complaint, frustration, disappointment, warning, failure, or clear criticism.

Example The app crashed twice and support never replied.

Neutral

Use for factual, descriptive, mixed, unclear, or non-emotional statements without a dominant sentiment.

Example The package was delivered on Tuesday afternoon.
